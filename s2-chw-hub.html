Community Health Hub

The current court system is complicated and punative, and it's not working well enough for tenants. In order to better facilitate Tenant/Landlord resolution, we feel their needs to be an alternative space to the traditional courtroom. We propose leveraging vacant storefronts within the communities most in need to create new spaces for restorative resolution.
