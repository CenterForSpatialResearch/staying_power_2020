Eviction Memory

The last strategy focuses on the experience of tenants who have been evicted from their homes. Since the Bronx Civil Housing court is a two-minute (2) walk from the Bronx Museum of the Arts, we wanted to use the museum as one of the hubs where floater community health workers would be located to collect the experience of the families who were not able to stop the eviction process. The main objective of this strategy is to promote awareness about the effects of evicting a tenant specially during a global pandemic. This effort would composed of oral stories that narrate the experiences a tenant wants to share about their apartment or being evicted.
